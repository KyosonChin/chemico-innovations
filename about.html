WHY CHEMICO ?

Chemico Innovations Sdn Bhd was founded by Mr. WK Chong in 1982. Start from a humble beginning until this day, Chemico Innovations is not only a supplier for some local companies but also a sole proprietorship of Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) for several multi-national companies for glue-base traps, thanks to our trusted and high-quality proprietary glues. Our special formulated in-house made glues that’s make us different from our competitors.


Throughout the years, Chemico Innovations had made a lot of improvement in the glue formulation and design in the base boards in order to suit the current market demand and have to fulfill customer needs which is to reduce toxic pesticide usage to the minimum.


Under continuous Research and Development (R&D), we had developed and specializes in producing non-toxic (non-insecticide) based pest control products. Our range of non-pesticide products that could ease end user problem start from rodent/rats’ adhesives glue type, cockroach glue traps, transparent sticky pads, house lizard (geckos) repellent, bird repellents, crawling and flying insect glue pads, double reflector insect light traps and many more.
